# Westbrook Public Library — Rooms

## Quiet study room
Single-person carrel with a desk lamp and an outlet. Holds up to two
people. Located on the second floor. Eight rooms available.

## Group study room
Whiteboard, large table, eight chairs. Located on the second floor.
Holds up to eight people. Four rooms available.

## Conference room
Wall-mounted display, conference phone, large oval table. Located on
the third floor. Holds up to twelve people. Two rooms available.

## Maker space
Workbenches, three-D printer, sewing machine, soldering station.
Located in the basement. Holds up to ten people. One room available.

## Auditorium
Tiered seating, projector, microphone, podium. Located on the ground
floor. Holds up to fifty people. One room available. Requires staff
setup, so booking lead time is at least three days.
