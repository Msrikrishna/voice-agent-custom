# Westbrook Public Library — Hours

## Regular hours
- Monday through Thursday: nine AM to nine PM
- Friday: nine AM to seven PM
- Saturday: ten AM to six PM
- Sunday: noon to five PM

## Holiday closures
The library is closed on New Year's Day, Memorial Day,
Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.
On Christmas Eve and New Year's Eve the library closes at three PM.

## Last booking start time
The last room booking each day must start at least one hour before
the library closes — for example, by eight PM on a weekday and by
five PM on Saturday.

## Holiday week bookings
Bookings are not accepted on closure days. If you book a slot that
later turns out to fall on a holiday, staff will contact you to move it.
