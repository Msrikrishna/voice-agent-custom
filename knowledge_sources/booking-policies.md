# Westbrook Public Library — Booking Policies

## Who can book
Any patron with an active Westbrook library card in good standing.
You will need the last four digits of your card to confirm a booking.

## Booking window
Rooms can be reserved up to fourteen days in advance. Same-day
bookings are welcome if a slot is open.

## Maximum duration per booking
- Quiet study room: two hours
- Group study room: two hours
- Conference room: four hours
- Maker space: three hours
- Auditorium: full day, up to eight hours

## Limits per patron
Each patron may hold at most two active bookings at a time across all
room types. A third booking will be refused until one is used or
cancelled.

## Cancellation
You may cancel or shorten a booking up to thirty minutes before the
start time at no charge. No-shows lasting more than fifteen minutes
forfeit the room and count toward a three-strike suspension.

## Food and drink
Covered drinks are allowed in study and group rooms. No food in any
room. The auditorium permits catered events only with prior staff
approval.

## Conduct
Quiet hours are enforced after eight PM on weekdays. Group rooms must
remain at conversational volume. Disruption may result in the room
being reclaimed by staff.

## Fees
All study, group, conference, and maker space bookings are free for
library card holders. Auditorium bookings carry a fifty dollar setup
fee for non-library events.
